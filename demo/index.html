How It Works

📱

Step 1 — On-Device Only (rule-based · no model download)

Raw GPS, movement speed, stop count, and Payone transaction history stay on the device. Rule-based NLP converts them into two plain-text summaries: a situation summary (what is happening right now) and a spending profile (who Mia is as a consumer — categories, typical spend, active hours, area). Coordinates, merchant names, exact amounts, and timestamps are stripped before anything leaves the browser.

☁️

Step 2 — Backend Context (FastAPI · Payone stub)

The server provides live Payone transaction volumes for all 4 nearby merchants — how busy each one is right now vs. their hourly average. Merchants below their activity threshold have trigger_active: true, meaning they need customers and are eligible to offer a discount.

🧠

Step 3 — LLM Reasoning + Selection (Groq · Llama 3.1 8B)

The LLM receives all three inputs in one prompt: the spending profile, the situation summary, and the merchant catalog with rules (max discount, min basket, preferred emotional framing). It reasons across who Mia is, what is happening now, and which merchant needs her most — then selects the best match and generates a personalised offer card. No template, no hardcoded rule — the selection and the copy are both written by the model.
